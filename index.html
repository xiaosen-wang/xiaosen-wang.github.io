Xiaosen Wang

 

Xiaosen Wang is a researcher at Huawei currently. He received his master degree (advised by Kun He) from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China in 2022. He recevied his Bachelor’s degree from Excellent Student ACM Class at School of Computer Science and Technology, HUST in 2019. He is generally interesetd in Machine Learning and Deep Learning, especially in Adversarial Examples in Computer Vision (CV) and Natural Language Processing (NLP).

I am always open to any cooperation. If you are interesting in working with me, just drop me an email!
