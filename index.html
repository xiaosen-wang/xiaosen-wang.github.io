Biography

My name is Xiaosen Wang, a second-year master student from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China. I am a member of John Hopcroft Lab at HUST, advised by Prof. Kun He. I am generally interesetd in Machine Learning and Deep Learning, especially in Adversarial Examples in Computer Vision (CV) and Natural Language Processing (NLP), Generative Adversarial Nets (GANs).
